VinnovateIT

​VinnovateIT is the official Innovation and Incubation Lab of the School of Information Technology and Engineering (SITE) at Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT), Vellore. Established in 2014, the organization aims to foster innovation by providing resources and mentorship to students interested in various technological domains.​

Mission and Activities:

VinnovateIT supports its members in developing projects both technically and entrepreneurially. The organization offers resources across multiple technologies and motivates members to excel in technical fields. By undertaking projects that are both technical and highly innovative, VinnovateIT guides members towards entrepreneurship, highlighting its long-term benefits.
